Local Cue Word is a fully offline AI image tagging tool that runs on your phone.

It uses ONNX models to perform local inference — no internet required, your images never leave your device.

Features:
- Local AI inference powered by ONNX Runtime
- Automatic prompt generation organized by quality, subject, appearance, scene, and action
- Auto-generated negative prompts for Stable Diffusion
- Model recommendation based on detection results
- Batch image recognition
- Tag translation (Chinese ↔ English)
- Multiple AI model choices
- Customizable themes with custom background images
- Tag favorites and history records
